## CHROME WEB STORE

**Name:** SlackGuard — Is Your Boss Watching?

**Short description (132 chars max):**
Find out instantly if your Slack boss can read your DMs. Shows a live warning banner inside Slack. Free, private, no data collected.

**Full description:**
Most Slack users don't know this: depending on your company's plan, your employer can export and read every DM, private channel message, and direct conversation you've ever sent — without notifying you.

SlackGuard scans your workspace the moment you open Slack and shows a clear, colour-coded warning banner at the top of the page:

🔴 HIGH RISK — You're on Business+ or Enterprise. Admins have a self-serve export tool for all messages including DMs. They can read everything.

🟡 MEDIUM RISK — You're on Pro. Admins can request exports under limited circumstances.

🟢 LOW RISK — You're on the Free plan. Export capabilities are very limited.

HOW IT WORKS
SlackGuard reads the workspace metadata that Slack injects into your browser on page load — the same data that tells Slack which features to show you. It checks your plan type, export settings, and whether any known monitoring apps (Hanzo, Smarsh, Global Relay) are installed.

PRIVACY FIRST
SlackGuard reads absolutely no message content. It never reads what you type. It sends zero data anywhere. Everything runs locally in your browser. No account required. No sign-up.

WHAT TO DO IF YOU'RE HIGH RISK
- Never share personal information, job search activity, salary discussions, or complaints about management on Slack
- Use Signal or WhatsApp for truly private conversations with colleagues
- Check your full workspace settings at slack.com/account/workspace-settings

100% free. No ads. No premium tier. Just information you deserve to have.

## FIREFOX ADD-ONS (AMO)

**Name:** SlackGuard — Slack Privacy Scanner

**Summary (250 chars max):**
Instantly shows whether your Slack workspace admin can read your DMs. Colour-coded warning banner inside Slack. Reads no message content. 100% local, no data collected, completely free.

**Description:**
Did you know your employer may be able to read every Slack DM you've ever sent?

Slack's Business+ and Enterprise plans give workspace admins a self-serve export tool that covers all messages — including private channels and direct messages. There is no warning inside Slack. There is no notification sent to employees. Most people find out too late.

SlackGuard fixes this by showing a permanent colour-coded banner inside Slack:

🔴 HIGH RISK — Admins on your plan can export all DMs right now
🟡 MEDIUM RISK — Exports possible under certain circumstances  
🟢 LOW RISK — Limited export capability, you're relatively protected

BUILT FOR PRIVACY
SlackGuard never reads your messages. It only checks your workspace plan type and settings — the same information Slack loads in your browser anyway. Nothing is sent to any server. No account needed. No tracking. Fully open to inspection.

WORKS AUTOMATICALLY
Install it, open Slack, and you'll see your risk level within seconds. No configuration required.

WHAT IT DETECTS
• Workspace plan type (Free, Pro, Business+, Enterprise Grid)
• Export tool status (enabled or disabled)
• Active message retention policies
• Known third-party monitoring apps (Hanzo, Smarsh, Global Relay, Proofpoint)

Free forever.
